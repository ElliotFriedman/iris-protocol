# Iris Protocol — Brand Identity

Apply when naming, positioning, or explaining Iris Protocol.

## Brand Essence

Iris Protocol is trustless embedded wallet infrastructure for AI agents. The brand communicates: *precision security*, *configurable control*, and *radical transparency*.

**Position:** Between institutional security credibility and the agentic economy's forward energy. Not a consumer app. Not a memecoin. The trust layer.

**Core metaphor:** The iris aperture — you open and close it, you control what passes through.

## Name Rules

Always **Iris** or **Iris Protocol**. Never:
- "IRIS" (not an acronym)
- "iris protocol" (capitalize both)
- "The Iris Protocol" (no article)

Code contexts: `iris-protocol` (kebab) or `IrisProtocol` (PascalCase).

## The Iris Metaphor (triple duty)

1. **Aperture control** — camera iris dilates/contracts to control light. User configures how much autonomy their agent has. Primary metaphor.
2. **Identity** — eye iris is biometric. Connects to ERC-8004 agent identity.
3. **Mythology** — Iris was the messenger goddess bridging gods to mortals. Protocol bridges human intent to agent action.
